TrialMatchAI – Clinical Trial Matching AI System

AI & Healthcare • 2026

TrialMatchAI is an AI-powered system that matches patients to suitable clinical trials. It analyzes patient data—such as age, diagnoses, and lab results—and compares it with active trials from ClinicalTrials.gov using machine learning and semantic search. The platform ranks trials by predicted eligibility and provides explainable reports to support physician decisions. By automating trial matching, it helps speed up enrollment and connect patients with potentially life-saving treatments faster.
